\section{Summary}

System Security Researcher with deep expertise in Trusted Execution Environments
(TEE) and system virtualization. Possesses hands-on experience in designing and
implementing trusted hypervisors (KVM with VFIO) and TEE frameworks (Intel SGX,
AMD SEV-SNP). Proficient in vulnerability analysis and developing custom fuzzing
systems for complex system software, including hypervisors (QEMU, Bhyve,
Hyper-V) and kernels (Linux, Windows, Android bootloader). Eager to contribute a
strong background in ARM and x86 architectures to the design and implementation
of next-generation secure SoC products.
